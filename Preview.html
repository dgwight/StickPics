Description
An iMessage app for creating stickers out of your photos. The easiest way to turn your friends face into a sticker.

Draw to clear away the background of a picture, when you are done save to your sticker collection to use anytime.

Hold stickers to peel them, then pinch to resize and place on message bubbles.
